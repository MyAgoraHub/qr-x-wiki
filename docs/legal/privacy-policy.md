# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 5, 2026**

## 1. Overview

QR-X ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. **QR-X does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.** All app data stays on your device at all times.

This Privacy Policy explains what device data the app accesses, why it is accessed, and what choices you have.

## 2. Data We Access

QR-X accesses data only on your device, only for the purpose of delivering app features. No data accessed by these permissions is sent to us or any third party.

| Permission | Required / Optional | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Camera | Required | Scan QR codes and barcodes in real time. Frames are processed on-device only and never stored or transmitted. |
| Location | Optional | Used only when you choose to generate a Location QR code from your current coordinates. Your location is embedded directly into the QR code on-device and never sent externally. |
| Contacts | Optional | Used only when you choose to embed contact information from your address book into a QR code. Contact data is never uploaded. |
| Storage / Photos | Optional | Used only when you save a generated QR code image to your device. |

Your scan and generation history is stored locally on your device. We have no access to it.

## 3. Data Sharing

**We do not share any data with third parties.** QR-X has no backend servers, no analytics SDKs, and no advertising networks integrated at this time.

QR codes you scan may contain links to external websites. We have no responsibility for or control over those external sites.

## 4. Advertising

**QR-X does not currently include any advertisements or ad SDKs.**

We may introduce optional advertising in a future release. If we do:

- This Privacy Policy will be updated to disclose exactly what data ad providers access.
- The Terms of Service will also be updated accordingly.
- You will be notified through an app update before any ad SDK is active.

## 5. Data Retention

All data (scan history, generated QR codes) is stored locally on your device. You control it entirely.

- You can clear scan history at any time in the app Settings.
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored app data from your device.
- We retain no copies of your data because we never receive it.

## 6. Your Choices

You can manage app permissions at any time through your device Settings.

- Revoking a permission disables the feature that depends on it; all other features continue to work normally.
- You can clear your scan and generation history from within the app at any time.

## 7. Data Security

Because all data stays on your device, the security of your data is primarily governed by your device's own security settings (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We do not transmit data, so there is no server-side risk.

## 8. Children's Privacy

QR-X is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children because we do not collect personal data from anyone.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We will update this Privacy Policy if the app's data practices change, particularly before introducing any third-party SDKs or advertising. The "Last Updated" date at the top will always reflect the latest version.
